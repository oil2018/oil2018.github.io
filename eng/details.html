International Reading Contest

Theme

This year the theme is Friendship. This topic will be amply developed during the preparatory stage.

Prizes

The winners are awarded books, arts objects, money, diplomas.

Time and Location

Period – September 1st – 5th

Venue – Constanta

Organizers: Ministry of National Education – Romania

Expenses

The travel expenses and the medical insurance are covered by the participants whereas the accomodation expenses during the olympiad are incumbent upon the organizers. All participants (both students and accompanying teachers are provided with full accommodation (meals and boarding – free of charge) from the 1st to 5th of September.
